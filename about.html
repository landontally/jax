About Jax Property Group

Although founded in 2025, Jax Property Group has ties to commercial real estate since 1984. Jax Property Group focuses on retail and light industrial properties in the markets we know best; primarily central and south-central Indiana. These markets allow us to operate our buildings using our vertically integrated strategy. Being a hands-on property owner, we strive for Landlord perfection while being opportunistic in our growth path.
