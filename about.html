About Krane Properties

Krane Properties was founded in 1977, originally specializing in Indiana University student housing. From there, the business bought its first commercial property in 1984, and since, has solely focused on commercial real estate expansion. Krane Properties focuses on acquiring properties in the Bloomington and Indianapolis markets, as we’re experts in these markets, and we’re able to service these buildings from our home office. Being a hands-on property owner, we strive for landlord perfection, and are opportunistic in our growth path.
