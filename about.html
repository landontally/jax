About Kamen Real Estate

At Kamen Real Estate, we are a family-owned and operated business based in the heart of Bloomington, IN. We've been serving the community since 1977, specializing in providing top-notch campus housing to Indiana University students and commercial real estate services throughout the state.

We take immense pride in being a part of the Bloomington community and beyond, offering over 70 stunning residential buildings and a range of commercial spaces. Our dedication to making a positive impact on the community is unwavering, and we are thrilled to continue serving the area with our warm and friendly approach to real estate.
